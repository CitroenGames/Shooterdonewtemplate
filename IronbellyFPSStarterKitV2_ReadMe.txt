ADD TO PROJECT
Click the Add to Project button in the Epic Launcher to add this pack to your project. You must have version 4.13 or higher installed.

ARM ANIMATIONS (ARMS ONLY)
Punch - Left Click
Push Door - Right Click
Sprint - Hold W + Shift
Jump - Space
Change Arm Texture - E (when next to the FP arm wall)

CHANGE FIRST PERSON ARM IN DEMO
1) In your Content Browser, go to Content > FirstPersonBPIronbelly > Blueprints 
2) Open the FirstPersonCharacterIronbelly_FPSStarterKit Blueprint 
3) Click on the Viewport tab  
4) Click on the FP arm mesh, these will be in idle animation mode 
5) On the right hand side of your screen, go to the Details tab > Mesh and select a new Skeletal Mesh from the drop down
6) Close the Blueprint  
7) Click the play button to view newly selected FP arms

ARM + WEAPON ANIMATIONS
Walk - W 
Sprint - Hold Shift + W 
Fire - Click Left Mouse Button  
Reload - R  
Aim Down Sights - Hold Right Mouse Button  
Aim Down Sights Walk - Hold Right Mouse Button + W 
Aim Down Sights Fire - Hold Right Mouse Button + Click Left Mouse Button  
Raise/Lower - Mouse Wheel Down
Switch Weapon - E (when next to weapon pedestal)
